First Drawn

First Drawn is a portrait dialogue with my father. Since 2019, I sketch him and he photographs me. It's a way of documenting time, being present, and connecting through creativity.

Psychology Illustrations

Illustrations interpreting psychological concepts, research methods, and the ethical implications of emerging technologies on human behavior and society. This series was created during my time at i2 Media Research, based at Goldsmiths, University of London.
