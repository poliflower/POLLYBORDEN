About Me

Welcome! I’m a multidisciplinary researcher and illustrator exploring how technology reshapes emotion, identity, and culture. My work blends critical inquiry with visual storytelling to question systems of visibility, control, and connection.

My interests include digital identity, AI ethics, immersive environments, and speculative emotional design. This site is a collection of my projects, both visual and conceptual.
